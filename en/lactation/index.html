What is a lactation counselor?

A certified lactation counselor by Healthy Children’s Center for Breastfeeding is a women trained to help mothers who meet difficulties with breastfeeding and/or to answer their questions.

When do you need help from a lactation counselor?

  • babies with latch-on problems or slow weight gain
  • babies with physical or developmental problems
  • premature infants
  • multiples
  • mothers with flat, inverted, or sore nipples
  • mothers with an insufficient milk supply
  • to weight your baby
  • for questions about pumping and milk conservation
  • mothers with specific medical conditions
  • weaning

All mothers who encounter problems with breastfeeding should call for help!

But if you have more complex breastfeeding issues, the lactation counselor will refer you to a International Board Certified Lactation Consultant.

When to contact me

You can join me during your pregnancy and we can set up a prenatal visit. During this visit, I can answer your questions about the following topics and more:

  • previous breastfeeding problems
  • breast reduction
  • premature baby
  • c-section

Or you can join me after the delivery and I will visit you at home to find a solution to your problem.

My focus as a lactation counselor  is to provide education, assistance and support to breastfeeding women.

Please note: not all doulas are certified.
When choosing one, make sure to check her experience, training and certification.
