A Few Words About Me

Since I arrived in Canada in 2001, I have been alternately a stay-at-home mom and a caregiver. The challenges I faced after the births of my two children inspired me to redirect my career and to obtain certification as a postpartum doula.

After a few years of practice, to be able to meet all the needs of the mothers who call me, I decided to become a lactation counselor. This new certification enabled me to acquire and enhance my breastfeeding knowledge and allows me now to help new mothers succeed in something close to their heart.

Helping new parents and sharing my knowledge and my experience with them is my vocation.

Please note: not all doulas are certified.
When choosing one, make sure to check her experience, training and certification.
